About us

A few years ago a group of friends from Stockholm, Sweden pulled out some decks in an old warehouse and hosted a rave. This went down as 'one of those nights' and is where Diaspora was born. The definition of A Diaspora reads: A group of people that is scattered across regions which are separate from its geographic place of origin. We found a home in Stockholm, but our roots reach far across different lands, oceans and continents. Our inspiration therefore comes in the clash of different cultures, and culminates into borderless sounds and ideas.

Our purpose

... is to create spaces both physically and digitally where the people of the diaspora can see, hear and feel the words, sounds and movements of the duality and the context that we exist in. We are a group of friends who are passionate about music and culture and we want to collaborate with like-minded people.
