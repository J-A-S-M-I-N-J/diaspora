About us

In early 2022, Mansa would meet with some friends and arrange evenings at a spot in central Stockholm where people would gather to listen to music, dance and socialize. The idea was to create a space where people with roots outside of Europe could meet and share their stories, music and culture. Thus, Diaspora was born. For us, music is a universal language that reaches out and connects people, and culture is the glue that binds us closer together.


The definition of A Diaspora reads: A group of people that is scattered across regions which are separate from its geographic place of origin. We love to rep our area code 08 - Stockholm, but our sound, our music, and our culture is a reflection of the roots and experiences that made us who we are. Our intent is then to collaborate with you, into creating more borderless sounds and ideas and show them to the world.


We're here to so that you can hear and feel the words, sounds and movements of a duality and the experience of living in a diasporal context. We used to only be live at events here in the north ... but now we have a digital platform, to be able to reach you, over there, who resonates in the same spirit as we. Something by Diaspora, for the Diaspora.
